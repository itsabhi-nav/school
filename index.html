Laboratory And Classes

Science Lab

Our lab is equipped with state-of-the-art equipment, including microscopes, Bunsen burners, and test tubes, to facilitate hands-on learning and experimentation. The lab also has a wide range of chemicals and materials available for use, allowing students to explore various scientific concepts in-depth. Safety protocols are strictly enforced in the lab to ensure the well-being of students and staff. The Udayan Public School science lab is an excellent resource for students, providing a practical learning environment where they can explore scientific theories and develop their skills in scientific inquiry.

Computer Lab

Computer Lab at Udayan Public School is a well-equipped facility with modern computers, printers, and other peripherals. It is designed to cater to the needs of students from primary to senior secondary levels. The lab is spacious and has a comfortable seating arrangement for students. It is staffed by experienced and skilled computer instructors who help students to acquire knowledge and skills related to computers and technology. The lab has high-speed internet connectivity, which enables students to access online resources for research and other purposes.

Library

It is housed in a spacious and airy room that provides a conducive environment for reading and learning. The library is equipped with a wide range of books, magazines, journals, and reference materials, catering to the needs and interests of students from different age groups and academic levels. The library subscribes to many academic and professional journals, keeping students and teachers up-to-date with the latest research and developments in their fields.

About Us

Udayan Public School is a well-known educational institution located in Japla, Jharkhand, India. The school is based on Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) and offers education from nursery to class XII.

The school has a spacious and modern campus with well-equipped classrooms, laboratories, libraries, and sports facilities. It has a team of experienced and dedicated faculty members who strive to provide a holistic education to students.
