About Us

Learning today, leading tomorrow.

Udayan Public School is a co-educational English medium school located in Japla, a town in the state of Jharkhand, India. The school is based on Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) and offers classes from Nursery to Class XII.

The school aims to provide quality education to its students and focuses on holistic development, including academic excellence, character building, and social responsibility. Udayan Public School has well-equipped classrooms, laboratories, libraries, sports facilities, and a computer center to facilitate the learning process of its students.

The school has a team of experienced and dedicated teachers who use innovative teaching methods to make learning enjoyable and effective. The curriculum is designed to cater to the individual needs of each student, and emphasis is given on developing critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

Apart from academics, the school also offers various co-curricular activities such as music, dance, art, and sports to develop the overall personality of its students. Udayan Public School also encourages its students to participate in community service programs to instill a sense of social responsibility in them.

Laboratory And Classes

Science Lab

Our lab is equipped with state-of-the-art equipment, including microscopes, Bunsen burners, and test tubes, to facilitate hands-on learning and experimentation. The lab also has a wide range of chemicals and materials available for use, allowing students to explore various scientific concepts in-depth. Safety protocols are strictly enforced in the lab to ensure the well-being of students and staff. The Udayan Public School science lab is an excellent resource for students, providing a practical learning environment where they can explore scientific theories and develop their skills in scientific inquiry.

Computer Lab

Computer Lab at Udayan Public School is a well-equipped facility with modern computers, printers, and other peripherals. It is designed to cater to the needs of students from primary to senior secondary levels. The lab is spacious and has a comfortable seating arrangement for students. It is staffed by experienced and skilled computer instructors who help students to acquire knowledge and skills related to computers and technology. The lab has high-speed internet connectivity, which enables students to access online resources for research and other purposes.

Library

It is housed in a spacious and airy room that provides a conducive environment for reading and learning. The library is equipped with a wide range of books, magazines, journals, and reference materials, catering to the needs and interests of students from different age groups and academic levels. The library subscribes to many academic and professional journals, keeping students and teachers up-to-date with the latest research and developments in their fields.

Smart Class

We integrated smart class technology into its classrooms, providing an innovative and interactive learning experience for students. The smart class is equipped with modern audio-visual equipment, such as projectors, smart boards, and speakers, to support the teaching and learning process. The use of smart class technology enables teachers to present complex concepts and ideas in a more engaging and understandable manner. They can display and annotate digital content such as videos, images, and diagrams, making learning more interactive and immersive. matter.
